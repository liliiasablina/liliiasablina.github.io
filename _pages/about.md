I am a postdoctoral researcher in European Studies at the Vienna School of International Studies (Diplomatic Academy Vienna) and Visiting Faculty at Central European University, with a background in political science and international relations. My research focuses on authoritarianism, transnational repression, political communication, propaganda, and computational text analysis.

## Research

My research examines how authoritarian states communicate, mobilize supporters, target political opponents, and influence political debate across national borders, as well as how these processes affect political dynamics within the European Union in both domestic and foreign policy.

I combine computational text analysis with qualitative and interpretive methods.

My current research projects focus on:

-   Russian transnational authoritarianism and diaspora politics
-   The dynamics of Eurosceptic politicization in Europe
-   Propaganda and disinformation
